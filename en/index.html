01 · About

I build learning.
I design experiences.

I'm Ben — a hands-on learning developer with more than three years of end-to-end experience, from the first needs-analysis meeting all the way to launch day. I build digital training programs for instructors using Storyline, H5P and studio-produced video; I've delivered B2B customer education; and I create interactive learning materials for the K-12 education sector.

I'm currently in the middle of an M.A. in Learning Technologies (AI track) at HIT — a program that puts me at the front edge of how AI is reshaping the way people learn. The foundation came from a dual-major B.A. in Hebrew Linguistics and English Literature at Ben-Gurion University, and that deep relationship with text, language and structure is still the compass that guides everything I build.

I work across Articulate Storyline and Claude Code — traditional authoring alongside code-based development. I run a studio, record in OBS, edit in DaVinci Resolve, and scope bilingual curricula.
