01 · About

I build learning.
I tell stories.

I'm Ben — a hands-on learning developer with more than three years of end-to-end experience, from the first needs-analysis meeting all the way to launch day. My work lives inside Israel's government programs and enterprise L&D departments, across a wide range of subjects and formats.

My background starts with language and literature — a double B.A. from Ben-Gurion University in Hebrew Linguistics and English Literature — and it carries me today through an M.A. in Learning Technologies (AI track) at HIT. Text, voice, and the way people actually learn are still my compass.

I'm as comfortable inside Articulate Storyline as I am in an open terminal with Claude Code. I run a studio, record in OBS, edit in DaVinci Resolve, scope bilingual curricula, and own the full instructor lifecycle — because real learning development is all of those things at once.
